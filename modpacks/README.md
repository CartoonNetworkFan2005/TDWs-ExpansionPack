# The Modpacks Folder

This is where you can drag and drop the characters and songs into their respective folders.
Make sure not to put them in the wrong one, or it won't work.

(Note that some engines, like Codename, utilize a "switch-to-mod" feature that may not feature all of the features in the separate mods when put together. To fix that remedy, the "For Codename" folders in either "Chars" or "Songs" will either be "light" (no songs or stages, just characters) or "heavy" (the full mod).)
